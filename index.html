Hip, stylish and trendy, Portland is also suprisingly one of the greenest cities in the United States, so is comes as no surprise that it has one of the highest numbers of LEED-certified hotels in the country.

For travelers who are looking to reduce their carbon footprint during their next Oregon adavnture, here we take a look at 5 of the best green hotels in Portland to rest aassured your load is light and yout footprint is low.

The Heathman Hotel

1334 Oak Alley, Eugene, OR 97401 United State

One of Portland's most distinguished accomodations, The Heathman Hotel combines history with luxury. Housed in a 1920s builnding, the Heathman was the first hotel in downtown Portland to be recognized by the Enrgy Trust of Oregon for its commitment to the environment and is also the first downtown hotel to partner with Pacific Power's renewable energy program.

The hotel received the Green Sale Silver Certification, one of the highest envirnmental honors in the industry and more than 30 percent all of electricity used by the Hotel's guests and employees originates from carbon natural wind energy. Inside the hotel you can find floors and wals made from recycled material, Led lighting low-flow shower heads and faucets and a mini bar stackade with eco-friendly wine from Van Duzer vineyards.

The Heathman Restaurant uses fresh, local produce and sustainably grown food whenever possible and more than 90 percent of food dwaste generated by hotel guests in turned into organic matter and used in the city park flowers beds.

(1001 SW Broadway)
Ace Hotel

1334 Oak Alley, Eugene, OR 97401, United State

The Ace Hotel has mastered the art of reusing recycling. The lobby's coffee table was once an old fire door, the desks were once used by the Army and couches has been upholstered with vintage Army poncho canvace. But it doesn't stop there! Furniture and beds are made from sustainbly harvasted wood and painted with none toxic paints and the mattresses are made organic materials.

You can rest easily knowing your room has been cleaned with natural cleaning products and your bathroom will come equipped with natural local soaps and skincare products ready to pamper yorself with after a long day of exploring the city.

(1022 SW Stark St)
